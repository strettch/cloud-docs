## How it works

When someone signs up using your referral link and tops up their account to the threshold, you both receive **25,000 RWF** in credits.

It's a simple loop: you share your link → they sign up and top up → you both get rewarded.

---

## Finding your referral link

Go to **Account Settings → REFER & EARN**. You'll see your unique referral link there — something like `https://cloud.strettch.com/auth/sig...`. Your referral code is embedded in the link, so there's nothing separate to copy. Just share the link directly.

---

## Tracking your referrals

On the **REFER & EARN** tab, you can see everyone who signed up using your link. For each person, you can see whether they've hit the top-up threshold and both become eligible for credits.

There's no progress indicator — you'll only see whether a referral has qualified or not.

---

## Receiving your credits

You don't need to go looking for your credits. When a referral qualifies, you'll see a **popup the next time you log in** asking which team to apply the credits to.

You can only apply credits to a team you **own**. If you own more than one team, you'll be able to choose between them at that point.

<Note>
The popup appears once on your next login after a referral qualifies. Make sure you select the right team — this is the only prompt you'll get.
</Note>

---

## What counts as qualifying?

The referred user needs to top up funds into a team they own. Once their cumulative top-ups on that team reach the 25,000 RWF threshold, both of you become eligible for the reward.

Simply signing up is not enough — a top-up has to happen.

---

## Frequently asked questions

**Is there a limit to how many people I can refer?**
No. You can share your link with as many people as you like.

**Can I apply credits to a team where I'm just an admin or member?**
No. Credits can only be applied to teams you own.

**What if the referred user tops up but hasn't reached the threshold yet?**
Nothing happens yet. The reward only triggers once their total top-ups reach 25,000 RWF on the team they own.

**What if I miss the popup on login?**
Contact [cloud@strettch.com](mailto:cloud@strettch.com) and the support team can help.
