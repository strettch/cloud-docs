## How does billing work?
Strettch Cloud operates on a prepaid credit system. You add funds to your team's wallet, and your resource usage is deducted from this balance daily.


## How is usage calculated?
We track your resource usage (like Virtual Machines) continuously.


* **Compute (VMs):** Usage is calculated daily for the previous day. We bill by the hour. If you use a VM for part of an hour, it is rounded up to the full hour.


* **Public IPs:** Billed monthly. If you hold a public IP at any point during the month, you are charged the monthly fee.

We currently accept payment using credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, UnionPay), debit cards. We also support direct debit from banks.

## When is my balance updated?

* **Top-ups:** Your balance increases as soon as we receive payment confirmation from IremboPay.

* **Usage Charges:** Your balance is debited once every 24 hours for the previous day's usage.

## What happens if my balance runs out?
If your balance is insufficient:

* **Past Due:** You will receive a reminder email.

* **Suspension:** If payment is not made, your resources may be suspended.

* **Termination:** Continued non-payment may lead to resource termination. We recommend keeping a positive balance to avoid service interruption.
