## What are audit logs?

Audit logs give you a record of actions taken within your team. Each entry shows what happened and when, so you can track changes and understand the state of your team's resources over time.

Logs are scoped to the current team. Switching teams shows the audit log for that team only.

---

## Accessing audit logs

Go to your team's dashboard and open the **Audit Logs** tab. Entries are listed in reverse chronological order — most recent first.

Each entry shows:

- **Who did What** — a plain-English description of what happened (e.g., "You joined Team Engineering Team")
- **Time** — how long ago the action occurred, grouped by date

---

## What gets logged

| Category | Example actions |
|----------|----------------|
| **Compute** | Instance created, instance restarted, instance deleted |
| **DNS** | DNS record created, DNS record updated, DNS record deleted |
| **Team membership** | Member invited, member removed, role changed |
| **Team details** | Team name changed, team settings updated |
