Once you've signed up, you can start deploying your applications on Strettch Cloud after selected the plan that suits your needs.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose your billing plan">
    After signing up, select the billing plan that best suits your needs and budget. This will determine your resource limits and available features.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up your team">
    You'll automatically have a default team created with you as the owner and member. This team serves as your workspace for managing projects and resources. You can learn more about [team management](/guides/teams) in our documentation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start scaling your infrastructure">
    With your team set up, you can now begin building your cloud infrastructure:
    - **Create computes**: Deploy virtual machines and containers
    - **Add custom domains**: Connect your own domains to your applications  
    - **Invite team members**: Collaborate by adding other developers to your team
  </Step>
</Steps>
