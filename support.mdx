## Before You Contact Support

To help us assist you more effectively, please:

1. **Check the documentation** - Many common questions are answered in our [guides](/guides/teams) and [FAQs](/faqs/paying-your-bill)
2. **Gather details** - Include error messages, screenshots, and steps to reproduce issues
3. **Provide context** - Share your account email, project name, or relevant configuration details
4. **Be specific** - Clear descriptions help us resolve issues faster

---

## Support Guidelines

### What to Expect

✅ **We will**:
- Respond to inquiries in a timely manner
- Provide technical assistance and troubleshooting
- Help resolve billing and account issues
- Consider feature requests and feedback
- Maintain professional and respectful communication

❌ **We cannot**:
- Provide custom development services
- Guarantee specific feature implementations
- Offer 24/7 immediate support (except for critical issues)
- Assist with third-party integrations outside our scope

### Your Responsibilities

To receive the best support experience:

- **Provide detailed information** - Include error messages, logs, and reproduction steps
- **Cooperate with our team** - Respond to follow-up questions and testing requests
- **Use self-service resources** - Check documentation and FAQs first
- **Maintain respectful communication** - We're here to help you succeed
